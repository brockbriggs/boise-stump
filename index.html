What We Do

Deep Root & Stump Grinding

We don't just shave the top off. We grind stumps 8 to 12 inches below grade, destroying the root crown so you can easily lay fresh sod, plant a garden, or reclaim your yard.

Woodchip Cleanup & Haul-Away (Optional)

Grinding a stump creates a surprisingly large pile of woodchips. We can rake them neatly into the hole for you to use as free mulch, or—for an additional fee—we can haul them completely away.
